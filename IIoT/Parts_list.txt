Parts required for the IIoT demo:

Raspberry Pi 3 or 4
Raspberry Pi power supply
MicroSD card, at least 8 GB
Dupont jumper wires
Electronics breadboard
LED - any colour
switch - I used this one, with a cut up jumper wire soldered to two of the legs: https://www.bitsbox.co.uk/index.php?main_page=product_info&cPath=116_134&products_id=903
AM2302 or DHT22 temperature and humidity sensor - I used this: https://www.bitsbox.co.uk/index.php?main_page=product_info&cPath=302_306&products_id=2478
2 x 1 kOhm resistor
1 x 220 Ohm resistor

A set of peripherals for the Rasbperry Pi is also useful. You can often but a bundle that includes everything apart from a screen.
